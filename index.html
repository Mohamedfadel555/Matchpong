Tutorial

▶ This ping pong game is somewhat difficult and you will find out for yourself.
▶ The rules are easy: whoever makes the ball hit the line behind the opponent scores a goal, and whoever scores 5 goals wins the game.
▶ But it is not that easy,The ball increases its speed every 20 seconds and also disappears, and you must anticipate the location of the ball so that the ball does not hit the line behind you and to distract attention. Every 45 seconds, the colors of everything change between black and white for 20 seconds, then the colors return to their normal state. That is all.
